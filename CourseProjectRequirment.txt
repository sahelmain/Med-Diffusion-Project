Course project requirement/guidance
The requirement of the midterm report and presentation is as follows:
1. Re-implement all methods in a selected paper;
2. Reproduce the experimental results for the selected paper;
3. Novel potential technologies to improve the experimental results;
4. At least 5 pages in IEEE double column format (see attachment).
The requirement of final report and presentation is as follows:
1. Implement all novel technologies;
2. Conduct experiments to compare with existing methods;
3. Refine/modify/redesign novel technologies to make the results of novel methods significantly
better than existing methods;
4. Perform experimental analysis on all experimental results;
5. At least 12 pages report in IEEE double column format.
